The Beginning

Who Is Natsuki Subaru?

An Ordinary Boy in an Extraordinary World

Natsuki Subaru is a 17-year-old shut-in who is suddenly transported from modern Japan into a vibrant, dangerous fantasy world. Without powers, wealth, or knowledge of where he is, he is utterly powerless by every conventional measure.

Yet he carries something rarer: an iron will, boundless empathy, and the most terrifying blessing in existence — the power of Return by Death. Every time he dies, the clock resets. And every time, he gets back up.

Character

What I Admire Most

01🔥

Unyielding Resolve

Subaru faces death endlessly and rises each time. He doesn't just endure suffering — he weaponizes it. Every loop is fuel for his determination to reach a future where everyone he loves survives.

02💔

Authentic Vulnerability

Unlike typical protagonists, Subaru breaks. He cries, screams, despairs — and that rawness makes him deeply human. His greatest arc is learning that admitting weakness is not defeat, but the very foundation of real strength.

03🛡

Selfless Love

His love for Emilia, Rem, Beatrice, and all those around him is never transactional. He gives everything — his sanity, his body, his life — not for reward, but because he cannot bear to see those he cherishes suffer.

04🌀

Courage Under the Curse

Imagine knowing you'll die again, knowing the pain is coming — and walking forward anyway. Subaru bears crushing forbidden knowledge alone, unable to tell anyone. Yet he persists, in silence, every single time.

05🌸

Growth Through Pain

From a self-absorbed shut-in to someone who lives entirely for others — Subaru's transformation is one of anime's most compelling arcs. He earns every step of his evolution through blood, grief, and radical self-reflection.

06⚔️

Tactical Brilliance

Without magic or a hero's body, Subaru fights with his mind. He uses knowledge from past loops to outmaneuver enemies and craft impossible victories — making him one of the most unconventional heroes ever written.

His Journey

Key Story Arcs

Arc 1 · Isekai Arrival

The First Death

Transported to another world and immediately killed in an alley, Subaru discovers Return by Death — a power that resets time to a checkpoint upon his death. What begins as a gift reveals itself quickly as an incomprehensible curse.

Arc 2 · Mansion of Witches

Desperate Loops at Roswaal Manor

Subaru fights an invisible curse that kills everyone in the mansion over and over. Each loop peels away more of his sanity. He watches allies die, powerless — until he learns to trust others, not just fight alone.

Arc 3 · Royal Capital

Pride Before the Fall

Drunk on determination and arrogance, Subaru publicly demands support for Emilia's royal selection and is utterly humiliated. This arc contains his lowest point and most pivotal breakdown, followed by the legendary Rem confession scene.

Arc 4 · Great Rabbit & Sanctuary

Against Impossible Odds

Facing the Great Rabbit and the Witch of Greed's trials, Subaru is stripped to nothing. What emerges is a warrior who has accepted death as a tool, and life as something worth fighting for with everything he has.

Arc 5 & Beyond

The Sin Archbishop Rises

Bearing the Sin of Pride, Subaru evolves into a strategic mastermind and emotional anchor for all factions. Every death, every scar endured has sharpened him into someone who doesn't just survive — he leads.

I'm not running away anymore. I'll use every death, every loop, every scar — to reach the future where you're smiling. That's my only goal. That's enough for me.

— Natsuki Subaru

Final Word

Why Subaru Matters

Natsuki Subaru is not a hero because of power. He is a hero because he chooses to be — every single time, in the face of every horror thrown at him. He is proof that the measure of a person is not what they are given, but what they refuse to give up. In a genre full of chosen ones, Subaru stands apart: a trembling, weeping, relentlessly human boy who decided love was worth dying for — infinitely.
